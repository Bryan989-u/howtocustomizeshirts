Frequently Asked Questions

Question Answer
What is the best HTV? There are different types of HTV, and different brands that offer great qualities. But personally, I like to use CAD CUT Ultraweed Heat Transfer Vinyl, which is sold through Stahls. I will provide the link to the product.
What temperature should I set my heat press? The temperature should be set depending on the instructions for your HTV and garment. For the HTV I mentioned above the instructions are as follows: Time 12 seconds, temperature 260F-300F, and peel hot.
What is the best HTV cutting machine? After extensive research, I found that the Cricut machine seems to be one of the best on the market, at least to start off. It is good for the price. It comes with the software, a sticky mat, needle, weeding device, and great size for standard designs.
How should I take care of the shirt after applying HTV? Try washing your shirts inside out, do not bleach, do not dry clean.
Instructions
What type of printer is used for DTG? It is usually an inkjet printer with water-based ink. There are multiple options to choose from, but the most common brands are Epson, Ricoh, and Brother GTX.
Does the design fade away quickly when I use DTG? Commonly, it should not wash away when worn less than 50 times. But a shirt that is cured well and by an experienced person should last for a while. It is also a good practice to take care of your shirt to make it last longer especially when you know the method that was used.
What images do you plan to use, and where will you use them? I plan to use images that I have taken for content creation for my business. I will use them on the homepage, HTV page, DTG page, Screen Printing page, and order custom shirts.
